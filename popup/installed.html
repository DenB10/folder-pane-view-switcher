Features


This add-on adds arrow buttons at the top of the folder pane which rotate back and forth through the various available folder views.

This add-on also causes Thunderbird to automatically switch to the "All Folders" view when you hover over the title above the folder tree for several seconds during a message drag & drop operation, and switch back automatically after the drop. In the add-on preferences, you can set the delay before the view is switched.

Finally, in the add-on preferences, you can select which views are accessible in the pop-up menu at the top of the folder list, and of those, which show up when you rotate through the views using the arrow buttons.
